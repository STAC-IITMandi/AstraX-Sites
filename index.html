Keynote Speakers

“The first step toward success is taken when you refuse to be a captive of the environment in which you first find yourself.” ~Mark Caine.
Presenting the gems in the field of everything related to science.

Dr. Nandivada Rathnasree

The director of Nehru Planetarium, Delhi, Dr. Rathnasree is an enthusiastic professional-amateur astronomer and works tirelessly for promotion of amateur astronomy in India.

Dr. Chandra Kant Mishra

Assistant Professor at Physics Department, IIT Madras. Prof. Mishra is a member of LIGO India and is a co-author of the paper titled "Observation of Gravitational Waves from a Binary Black Hole Merger" on the basis of which Nobel Prize in Physics 2017 was awarded. IIT Mandi will be hosting the 2nd out of 40 Indian Contributors to that discovery.

Juan Luis Cano Rodríguez

(Remote)

An Aerospace Engineer, currently works at Satellogic. Juan is a Python Professor at Instituto Empresa and Barcelona Technology School. He has published several open source packages and is the chair of Python España association.

Dr. Redouane Boumghar

(Remote)

A data scientist, Dr. Boumghar is a research fellow in Data Mining and Artificial Intelligence at European Space Agency and AI and Robotics Mentor at NASA Frontier Development Lab.
